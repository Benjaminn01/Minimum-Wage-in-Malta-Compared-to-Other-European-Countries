Minimum Wage in Malta Compared to Other European Countries

Malta has a minimum wage of €221.78 per week in 2025 which is not the highest nor the lowest when compared to other European countries. Countries like Luxembourg, Germany and Netherlands lead the list with highest minimum wages. While Slovakia, Latvia and Bulgaria stand at the bottom with lowest minimum wages.

Highest Minimum Wage in Europe

Luxembourg has the highest minimum wage across all EU nations.

  • For 18 years old and above, it’s €14.86 to €15.24.

  • For 17 to 18 year old workers, it’s €11.88 to €12.19.

  • For 15 to 17 year old workers, it’s €11.14 to €11.43.

Lowest Minimum Wage in Europe

Bulgaria has the lowest minimum wage across all EU nations, standing at BGN 6.49 (approximately €3.32) in 2025.

Where Does Malta Stand?

Malta stands amongst the EU nations with a comparatively low minimum wage at a rate of €5.54 per hour. With additional benefits, bonuses, and a comparatively low cost of living, it is possible to live a comfortable life in Malta.
